Supply Convergence Proof

Theorem 6.2.3 (Supply Convergence): The total supply converges to exactly 21 million BTC.

Proof: The total supply can be expressed as a sum of geometric series. For each halving period $k$ (where $k = \lfloor h/H \rfloor$), the subsidy is $50 \times C \times 2^-k$ for $H$ consecutive blocks.

$$lim_{h o infty} ext{TotalSupply}(h) = 21 imes 10^6 imes C$$

The total supply is:

$$ ext{TotalSupply}(infty) = sum_{k=0}^{63} H imes 50 imes C imes 2^{-k} = H imes 50 imes C imes sum_{k=0}^{63} 2^{-k}$$

Since $\sum_k=0^63 2^-k = 2 - 2^-63 \approx 2$ for large $k$:

$$ ext{TotalSupply}(infty) approx H imes 50 imes C imes 2 = 210,000 imes 50 imes 10^8 imes 2 = 21 imes 10^6 imes 10^8 = 21 imes 10^6 imes C$$

Source: Orange Paper, Theorem 6.2.3 (GitHub)
